◆ test_BundleController_updateItem_remove()

void com.example.JO2024.Controller.BundleCartControllerTest.test_BundleController_updateItem_remove ( ) throws Exception
inline

Test case to verify the functionality of removing an item from the cart in the BundleCartController.

This test method performs the following steps:

  1. Retrieves a customer by email and generates a valid access token.
  2. Creates a new bundle with a quantity of 1 and adds it to the cart.
  3. Verifies that the cart is not empty after adding the bundle.
  4. Calls the endpoint to remove the item from the cart with a negative quantity.
  5. Asserts that the response status is OK and the message indicates successful removal.
  6. Verifies that the cart is empty after removing the item.

Exceptionif an error occurs during the test
