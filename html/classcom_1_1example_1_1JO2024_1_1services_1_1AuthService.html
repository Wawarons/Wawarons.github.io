Detailed Description

Service that encapsulates authentication-related functionalities.

This service provides methods for registering a new customer, confirming customer email, generating a new CustomerFullDTO object, generating a cookie with an access token, adding a cookie to the client, deleting a specific cookie from the client, and logging out the user.
