\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Service that encapsulates authentication-\/related functionalities.

This service provides methods for registering a new customer, confirming customer email, generating a new Customer\+Full\+DTO object, generating a cookie with an access token, adding a cookie to the client, deleting a specific cookie from the client, and logging out the user.
